About

Specialties

Personal Trainer Chris Wheeler caters to all levels of fitness, excels in new and efficient training and builds a program around each client. Our programs consist of a short personal diet review, planning an individual exercise program and establishing a road map for success and results. Chris Wheeler provides smart, innovative and targeted training in the gym. He has vast experience not only in training techniques, but also has worked in weight loss research.

History

Established in 2010.

Chris Wheeler has a strong background in training and experience with weight loss and body fat reduction. He is a former collegiate soccer player and has applied years of training to help clients reach goals quickly and efficiently. The results he has attained for his clients are exceptional. Chris Wheeler spent 7 years as the General Manager of the Natural Health Medical Center where he ran the nutrition/weight loss program.
