About Me

Specialties

Personal Trainer Chris Wheeler caters to all levels of fitness, excels in new and efficient training, and builds a program around each client. Programs include a personal diet review, individual exercise planning, and a roadmap for success. Chris Wheeler provides innovative and targeted training, drawing from vast experience and weight loss research.

History

Chris Wheeler has a strong background in training and experience with weight loss and body fat reduction. He is a former collegiate soccer player and has applied years of training to help clients reach goals quickly and efficiently. The results he has attained for his clients are exceptional. Chris Wheeler spent 7 years as the General Manager of the Natural Health Medical Center where he ran the nutrition/weight loss program.

Meet the Business Owner

Chris Wheeler has become an expert in smart, targeted training and weight loss after more than 10 years in the field. He brings a new dimension to the weight loss puzzle, emphasizing the importance of food, elimination, targeted cardio, and weight training. His journey and experience have helped him break the weight loss code and help thousands of people.
